Transit Center Plaza Project

Overview

  • Project Description: This is a housing and mixed use project that uses an existing on grade parking lot owned and operated by the City of Santa Barbara. Central to the concept is the use of a fully automated robotic parking garage to replace existing parking and provide additional parking for the proposed housing. Additionally, the redevelopment of a portion of State street with mixed use housing and a paseo that cuts through to State Street from the open space proposed for the transit plaza. The number of units proposed is between 200 and 400 depending on the type and size of unit.
