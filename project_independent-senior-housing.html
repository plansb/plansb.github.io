Independent Senior Housing

Overview

  • Project Description: The Public Policy Institute of California Estimates that, “The total number of adults age 65 and older is projected to grow from 5 million in 2014 to 8.6 million in 2030 in California.” This housing project would allow for Independent Senior Housing in a livley Downtown Santa Barbara setting. On the gorund level a new paseo and boutique commercial spaces embrace and celebrate the artistic scene that has been a part of this block of State Street for decades.
